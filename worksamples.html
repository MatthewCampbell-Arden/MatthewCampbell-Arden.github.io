Projects

Here are some of the projects that I have run:

Metal Trophy - Stage 4

The Metal trophy unit is built for stage 4 Technology Mandatory Metalwork. The unit is designed to allow students to interact with design and production elements

Rubberband Racer - Stage 4

The Rubberband Racer project is designed to be run with Technology Mandatory Engineering. The project introduces students to a simple engineering project, while allowing them to trial testing and iteration

Jewellery design - Stage 5

This Project allows students to design and produce their own jewellery using a variety of materials.

Jewellery Box - Stage 5

This Timber project is designed for Stage 5 Timber, and has a focus on joinery and precision

Chopping Board - Stage 5

This project is made for both Design and Technology and Timber. Students are required to design and fabricate their own piece, while learning skills like project management

Photoframes - Personal

This project was made as a gift for my coworkers at the end of the year
